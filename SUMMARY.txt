Summary: In 2019, GMC, Jaguar, and Satern had disproportionate high losses, with Buick having the highest average loss among top 10 car makes. Black had the most losses and was the most sold color, followed by Champaign and Burgendy. March was the busiest month for sales, with a 47.75% decrease compared to January, which was the slowest month. Friday was the busiest day of the week, with Sunday being the slowest. There was a trend of possible statistically insignificant spikes in losses on the first day of the month, particularly later in the evening.
